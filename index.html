Assured & Trustworthy AI Research Lab

UIUC CS · Advisor: Prof. Huan Zhang

Jan 2026 - Present

Working on simulation-grounded LLM training and evaluation workflows for analog circuit design, with a focus on turning circuit generation into a testable AI-for-EDA closed loop.

  • Developing task definitions and automated evaluation pipelines for specification-conditioned SPICE netlist generation, including sanity checks, Bayesian optimization-based sizing, PySpice simulation, testbench execution, and metric extraction.
