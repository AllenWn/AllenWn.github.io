Internship · Software Engineer Intern · Timeloit Technology Company · May 2023 - Aug 2023

Business Middle-Office Refactoring and CI/CD-based Engineering Optimization

Improving an enterprise backend platform through modular refactoring, dependency injection, reusable component design, and CI/CD-based deployment workflow optimization to enhance maintainability and development efficiency.

This internship focused on backend system refactoring and engineering optimization for an enterprise business platform. In enterprise software development, multiple customer-facing systems often share similar backend capabilities, such as user management, permission control, data interfaces, workflow configuration, and common service modules. Without a reusable platform layer, these functions can easily become duplicated across projects, leading to higher maintenance costs, tighter coupling, and slower development cycles.

The work centered on improving the modularity, maintainability, and extensibility of the company’s backend platform. The project involved analyzing existing system structure, identifying duplicated logic and tightly coupled modules, and assisting with module decomposition based on the single-responsibility principle. It also included exposure to dependency injection, reusable component design, interface standardization, and CI/CD-based deployment workflows.

This experience provided hands-on exposure to real-world enterprise backend engineering practices. It helped build an understanding of how modular design, code reuse, system refactoring, and automated deployment can improve software stability, development efficiency, and long-term maintainability in production-oriented business systems.
